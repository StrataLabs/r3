    How to Signup online?

    JustBooks provides you the option to Sign up online without any need to visit the branch. Now, get a membership at your ease and enjoy reading with JustBooks CLC.

    Step 1: Click on 'Sign up'

    Step 2: Choose the membership plan

    Step 3: Choose the branch nearest to your location

    Step 4: Fill out the Sign up form and click on 'Make Payment'

    Step 5: Make your payment through Credit Card/Debit Card/Net Banking

    Step 6: Click on the confirmation link sent to your email address

    You have successfully created your account with us. The Introduction package including your membership card would be sent to your address.

    How to Sign In? (For the first time users)

    You need to create a password and confirm your email address in order to Sign In

    Step 1: Click on 'Sign In' and then on 'Don't have a Password'

    Step 2: Input your email address and click 'Verify'

    Step 3: Click on 'Change my Password' in the email sent to you

    Step 4: Input your new password and click on 'Change my Password'

    Step 5: Sign In with your email address and the new password

    You have successfully signed in to your account .

    How to Order Online?

    You need to Sign In to your account before you can order for books online If this is your first time visit, kindly follow the procedure on 'How to Sign In'

    Step 1: Select the book(s) which you would like to read & click on 'Add to cart'

    Step 2: Repeat the Step 1 to add more number of books to your cart

    Step 3: Click on Check Out to confirm the book(s) to be rented

    Step 4: Click on Rent Now

    You have successfully placed an order for the book(s)

    How to Order Pickup?

    The books which are delivered to your home would also be picked up from your home at the next delivery.

    Step 1: Login In to your account

    Step 2: Click on Bookshelf, which is on the right top corner of the page

    Step 3: Click on 'Currently Reading' to view Book(s) or Magazine(s) issued to you

    Step 4: Click on the link 'Place Pickup Order' corresponding to each book.

    You have successfully placed Pickup order for the book

    How to search for a Book?

    You can search for a book with its Title/Author?/ISBN by simply typing in the search bar on the website. Titles matching the search will be displayed below.


    How to Add Books to Wishlist?

    Wishlist serves as a reference list for you to order interested titles at a later point in time.

    Step 1: Search for the Book(s) which you would like to add to wishlist by following the procedure on 'How to search for a Book'.

    Step 2: In the title page, click on Add to Wishlist

    You have successfully added the book(s) to your wishlist

    How to Order book(s) from the Wishlist?

    The book(s) in the wishlist can be ordered by following the below procedure.

    Step 1: Go to the tab Bookshelf

    Step 2: Click on 'Books you want to Read' where you will find all the books you have added to Wishlist

    Step 3: Click on 'Add to Cart' corresponding to each book

    Step 4: Click on Check Out, if you have added all the books you want to read to the cart

    Step 5: Click on Rent Now

    You have successfully order for the book from the wishlist

    How to Recommend Books?

    The book(s) which are not available with JustBooks can be recommended for procuring. The processing of such recommendation is at the sole discretion of JustBooks.

    Step 1: Go to the tab 'Bookshelf'

    Step 2: Click on 'My Recommendations'

    Step 3: Click on 'New Recommendation'

    Step 4: Search the title of the book in the search bar

    Step 5: Select the appropriate book and click on 'Proceed'

    Step 6: Click on 'Recommend' after verifying the details

    If the book is not among the list,


    Step 1: Click on 'Not found'

    Step 2: Enter the requested details and click on 'Recommend'

    You have successfully recommended for the book.

    How to check Availability of a book?

    Step 1: Search the title, for which you want to check availability

    Step 2: Click on Check Availability below the title. The City & Branch wise availability would be displayed to you.

    How to search for Regional books?

    You may search for regional book by either using the search bar (search with Title/Author) or by picking your language under the tab Catalog.


    How to Upgrade/Change Plan?

    Step 1: Sign in to your account

    Step 2: Go to the tab 'Accounts'

    Step 3: Click on the link 'Upgrade' next to the Plan on the page 'Membership Details'

    Step 4: On the next page, select the Plan you would like to upgrade to and click 'Continue'

    Step 5: Confirm your mode of payment and click on 'Continue'

    Step 6: You will be redirected to the payment page

    Step 7: Make the payment using your Credit Card/Debit Card/Net Banking

    You have successfully upgraded your account

    How to renew membership?

    Step 1: Sign in to your account

    Step 2: Go to the tab Accounts

    Step 3: Click on the link 'Renew' next to the Renewal date

    Step 4: Click 'Continue' on the next page and you will redirected to the payment page

    Step 5: Make the payment using your Credit Card/Debit Card/Net Banking

    You have successfully renewed your membership

    How to edit your contact details?

    Step 1: Login to your account

    Step 2: Go to the tab Accounts

    Step 3: Click on Contact Details in the left menu bar

    Step 4: Click on Edit in the contact details page

    Step 5: Update your contact details and click on Save

    You have successfully updated your account details

    How to Unsubscribe?

    Step 1: Login to your account

    Step 2: Go to the tab Accounts

    Step 3: Click on Privacy Settings in the left menu bar

    Step 4: Uncheck the options which you would like to unsubscribe from and click on Save

    You have successfully unsubscribed

    How to change Password?

    Step 1: Sign in to your account

    Step 2: Go to the tab 'Accounts'

    Step 3: Click on 'Change Password' on the left menu bar

    Step 4: Input your 'New Password' and the 'Current Password' to confirm the changes and click on Update

    You have successfully changed your password

    How to Order Magazines?

    Unfortunately, we don't have a catalog for Magazines online. You will need to order for the magazine(s) either by calling or visiting your branch.


    How to Subscribe Holiday?

    Step 1: Login to your account

    Step 2: Click on the tab Account

    Step 3: Click on the link Subscribe holiday?

    Step 4: Click on the button New Holiday Plans?

    Step 5: Select the Number of months, Holiday start date and click on Submit

    Step 6: If you have free slots of holiday available, then the Payable amount would be “0” and simply click on Apply holiday

    Step 7: Else if you don’t have free slots or are exhausted with the free slots, click on Make payment and you will be redirected to the CC Avenue.

    You have successfully initiated a Subscription Holiday.
    Note: The free subscription holidays available can be carried over to the next term only if you renew your membership before the expiry date else the available holidays would be lost.

    How to Cancel/End Subscription Holiday?

    Step 1: Login to your account

    Step 2: Click on the tab Account

    Step 3: If the holiday is Inactive, Click on the link Subscribe holiday? Else if the holiday is Active, Click on the link End holiday?

    Step 4: On the next page, click on the link Click here to end

    Step 5: Click OK to re-confirm

    You have successfully Cancelled/Ended the holiday

    How to change the branch?

    You may change your home branch by visiting either your current home branch or the branch which you would like to change to


    What do I do if I lost my membership card?

    You will need to visit your branch and place a request for a new membership card after paying Rs.100 towards the lost card


    What do I do if I lose a book/magazine?

    You will need to visit your branch to report the lost Book/Magazine and get it removed from your currently issued books list.
